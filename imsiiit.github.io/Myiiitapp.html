My IIIT App

About

Currently available for Android (with an iOS version coming soon), the app is designed to provide an engaging user experience for accessing various features of the institute websites (especially IMS) for students, staff & faculty at IIIT Hyderabad, along with some helpful resources. Users can use the app without needing a VPN.

User Guide

  • Further instructions about the app are available at https://gitlab.iiit.ac.in/instituteapps/docs, along with the User Guide.
  • In case you get any unauthorized access notification, please immediately block the app using the button below and change your institute email password. You can unblock the app later after changing your password.
  • Please fill the feedback form available below to help us improve the app and add more features.

Instructions to use the My IIITH mobile app

  • Click on the Download from Playstore button, and install from there.
  • Our app is compatible and tested with Android versions up to 9.0 (Pie) and above. We recommend using the latest version of our app to ensure you have the most secure experience.
  • In case of any unknown logins into the app with your account, you can block the app from the button below.
  • In case you have any issues, please write back to IMS Support Team (ims-support@iiit.ac.in).
